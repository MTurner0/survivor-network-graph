No season is an island: The many returning contestants of Survivor

Forty-two seasons of the American reality television show Survivor have aired, and contestants have been returning since season 8 (which aired in 2004). In this graph, vertices represent seasons. To see a season's name, simply brush over the graph (i.e. drag your cursor or your finger across the screen). An edge (or line) between two seasons indicates that at least one contestant played on both seasons. The thicker the edge, the more contestants played on both seasons.

Data from Dan Oehm, processed in R.
